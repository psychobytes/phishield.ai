About Phishield AI

Phishield AI is an advanced cybersecurity solution designed to protect users from phishing attacks. Utilizing the latest artificial intelligence technology, Phishield AI can detect and prevent phishing threats with very high accuracy.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide an extra layer of security for individuals and organizations in protecting their personal data and sensitive information from phishing attacks. We believe that with the right technology, cyber threats can be significantly minimized.

Our Team

We are a team from Group 20 who created a capstone project at SKILVUL titled "Phishing URL Detector". We hope that the website we developed can prevent URLs containing phishing, thereby protecting users from phishing threats. We are committed to ensuring our product is always up-to-date and effective in combating the latest threats.
